Frequently Asked Questions

Corporate conferences see a lot of delegates from out of town. Again, accommodation could be a challenge for visiting delegates. Conference venues that come with comfortable rooms are the best way to solve the problem. Of course, the zero commute time is a bonus.

Check out the capacity of the venue and make sure it is more than enough to accommodate the number of attendees you are expecting for the conference. The seating should not be cramped, and guests should have enough breathing room to mix and mingle.

Every conference will have a use for AV systems, overhead projectors, lecterns, and other paraphernalia. For more involved business conferences, you may require computers, wireless internet connections, even printers and fax machines. Check if the venue can provide them.

Make sure that the cost is within your event budget, of course. Be very clear on what items are covered by the fees and if there are any hidden fees or extra charges for special amenities you may require.
